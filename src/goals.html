Here are 10 tips for setting goals

  1. Use the SMART framework to create clear and actionable goals:
    Specific
    Measurable
    Achievable
    Relevant
    Time-bound
    This makes your goal concrete and trackable, instead of vague and overwhelming.
  2. Write them down
  3. Break your goals into smaller steps
  4. Set deadlines
  5. Understand your why
  6. Track your progress
  7. Be flexible
  8. Visualize success
  9. Share your success with others
  10. Celebrate milestones
